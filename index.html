About

A small
creative unit.

Together we form a small creative unit, developing projects that unite artistic research and experiments. Our focus is on poetic, site-sensitive works that invite audiences into experiences of memory, place, and transformation.

Kristina Kubrina
Co-founder · Creative Producer · Multimedia Artist

Builds visual systems end-to-end — art direction, production, R&D, pipeline design, and coordination of teams and vendors. Focused on making ambitious ideas technically real in specific site conditions.

Ivan Maksimov
Co-founder · CG Artist · Architect

Works across architecture, sculpture, and digital media — from location research and concept development to technical direction and implementation. Strong systems thinking: designs frameworks that connect space, form, and generative visuals.

Some major projects were made as part of previous teams; media is not published due to IP rights.
